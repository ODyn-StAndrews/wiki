# Funding opportunities

You will find here some resources and links for funding opportunities available and appropriate for the ODyn group. This include both opportunities to come to St Andrews either as a PhD student, a postdoctoral research associate, or a collaborator, as well as support for travel, meeting atttendance, and small research projects for existing group members.

For both prospective and matriculated graduate students, the [University Fees and Funding portal](https://www.st-andrews.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/fees/) is a good starting point. You can search here both for scholarship funding to attend the university, and for travel and research grants while studying here.
