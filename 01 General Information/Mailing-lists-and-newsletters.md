# Mailing lists and newsletters
A collection of mailing lists relevant to ocean and climate dynamics.

# Newsletters

**Natural Environment Research Council (NERC) updates**
- Posting predominantly about jobs and upcoming courses
- Approximately weekly
- [Subscribe](https://public.govdelivery.com/accounts/UKNERC/subscriber/new?preferences=true#tab1)

**UK Met Office**
- A number of different newsletters on different topics of interest, e.g. wearther, climate, etc.
- Approximately weekly.
- [Subscribe](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk/about-us/news-and-media/media-centre/subscribe-to-email-alerts)

**US Climate Variability (CLIVAR) newsgram**
- All things climate and climate variability, centered on US activities.
- Monthly postings highlighting special reports, upcoming events, conferences, jobs, webinars.
- [Subscribe](https://usclivar.org/get-involved)

# Mailing lists
These mailing lists generally include postings on jobs and PhD opportunities, as well as some specialised upcoming workshops and seminars. They are not curated (though they are moderated), meaning that users post to the list directly. It is recommend selecting to get posts as a "daily digest" if possible.

**Geophysical Fluid Dynamics (GFD) list**
- Posts about jobs, seminars, workshops
- [Subscribe](https://mailman.whoi.edu/mailman/listinfo/gfd-list)

**Cryolist**
- All things cryosphere, including sea-ice, glaciology, etc. 
- Often also high-latitude oceanography/climate
- Posts about jobs, seminars, workshops
- [Subscribe](https://lists.cryolist.org/mailman/listinfo/cryolist)

**ES Jobs**
- Jobs and opportunities in Earth Sciences
- [Subscribe](https://mailman.ucar.edu/mailman/listinfo/es_jobs_net)

**Met Jobs**
- Jabs and opportunities predominantly in atmosphere and ocean sciences
- [Subscribe](https://maillists.reading.ac.uk/scripts/wa-READING.exe?INDEX) (search for MET-JOBS)
