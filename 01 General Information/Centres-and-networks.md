# Centres and networks
A compilation of centres and virtual networks focused on ocean and climate research.

**[Association of Polar Early Career Scientists (APECS)](https://www.apecs.is/)**  Facilitating and highlighting opportunities for early career sciences working on anything concerning the high latitude regions (predominantly Arctic). They host webinars, highlight upcoming events, and have a job opportunities page.

**[Joint Exploration of the Twilight Zone Ocean Network (JETZON)](https://jetzon.org/)** Studying biological and physical processes in the ocean's twilight zone. They have ~monthly "open calls" involving one or two short talks from invited speakers. You can sign up to the mailing list [here](https://forms.office.com/pages/responsepage.aspx?id=1LH6uCPtgEGZAC0qeIDpugFrk6z9Z8hOt45XlM-vrpVUMVA2SEpPWUlFREtMUkpQNkJPNjRRWDJHRi4u&route=shorturl), and recordings of past talks are [here](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLd-85xguXhYhnt47ai1UzluP0I914tidc).
